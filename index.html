What is Salsa?

My Favorite Salsa Moves

Salsa is a lively and energetic dance style that originated in the Caribbean, particularly in Cuba and Puerto Rico. It combines elements of Afro-Cuban dance with influences from other Latin American dances. Salsa is known for its fast-paced footwork, intricate partner work, and vibrant music.

Salsa is the name for Latin American dances that are danced to salsa music. Salsa is one of the most popular types of Latin dance that is practiced worldwide, and is typically danced with a partner, although there are elements of solo footwork as well.
